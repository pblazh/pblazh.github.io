Cannes predictions

Client: Leo Bernett Ukraine

Task

The task was to create an interactive website for advertisers — fans of the Cannes Lions International Festival of Creativity who want to try themselves as the jury. Users could bet on contenders and as a bonus get their movies from a photo booth and compare a reaction of minds and hearts.

Solution

I have to create a website for an extremely demanding audience since the most favorite time killer for folks in the advertising community is to find flaws in the creations of colleagues. The site should be light, cross-platform, with an accurate animation.

For implementation, I chose Backbone as a basis and my favorite library from Greensocks for animation. I also used Jinja. It might be an overkill but I believe it is the most convenient of all available JavaScript template solutions. In order to get the maximum coverage camera capturing and work with a clipboard were duplicated in HTML5 and Flash.

The project is made using HTML5 and Flash technologies.
