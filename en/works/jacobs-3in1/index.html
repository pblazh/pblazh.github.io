JACOBS 3IN1

OPEN YOUR ANIMAL

OPEN YOUR ANIMAL

http://jac.rein.dev.studio7.com.ua/

Task

Create a promotional website for the campaign ”Jacobs 3in1. Taste with your character“, where each user can answer the questions and find out what kind of coffee Jacobs 3in1 taste for him.

Result

We have created a branded website where users have discovered an animal in itself. All you need — to answer the question, upload a photo, choose an animal and get your collage, which you can share with friends.

The project was developed using Flash technology.
