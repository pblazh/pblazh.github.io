The Guide

Agency: BBDO Ukraine and Proximity Ukraine

http://povodyr.com/

Task

To develop a website based on the principles of the inbound marketing for the Ukrainian film ”The Guide“ following the creative idea and design layout by BBDO Ukraine and Proximity Ukraine agencies

Solution

As the key task was a lightness and reliability, so the website offers creative and simplified access to the content. Another decision was to develop a website with a minimum of JavaScript and no server side at all.
