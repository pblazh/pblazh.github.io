NON STOP

website for energy drink

Client: New products

website for energy drink

http://non-stop.ua/

Task

Create maximally simple product web site for an energy drink Non-stop and get out to the target audience an idea: ”Non-stop is an infinite source of energy“

Solution

In one click user feels the mad rhythm of the city, choose a lifestyle of non-stop, as well as its corresponding drink — Non-stop Day or Non-stop Night.

The site should load fast has a low footprint end looks similar to different devices. For realization was chosen a simple design with few images and video background using Youtube. I chose also a simple and reliable stack of technologies MarionetteJS, SASS, AMD and bundling and assembling using Make.

The project is made using HTML5 (Backbone) technology.
