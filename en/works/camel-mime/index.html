GUESS, WHAT MIME SHOWED

Client: JTI, Agency: Peppermint interactive

Task

Develop mechanics

Re-invent popular game ”Crocodile“ for replenishment database of customers and engage them in communication with the brand. People come up with the words for each other and guessed them through pantomime.

Solution

There were filmed about 90 scenes — 3 for each letter of the alphabet. Big amount of video and also that there were a promo part and a personal area caused quite a big size of the project. Furthermore, the project was starting in a few steps and was developed by a distributed team.

In this project, I designed a modular architecture with an independent loading of separate modules and communication between them. I also made the promo part and a personal cabinet.

The project was developed using Flash technology.
