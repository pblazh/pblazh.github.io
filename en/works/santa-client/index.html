SANTA CLIENT

interactive Christmas card

Client: portal about creative and advertising – adme.ua, Agency: Peppermint interactive

interactive Christmas card

http://santaclient.com/

Task

Develop a New Year's greeting for visitors of advertising portal ADME.UA.

Solution

Everyone is waiting for Santa Claus with gifts for the new year. But ad agencies are waiting for confirmation budgets. We have created a virtual Santa client meeting room, where anyone could present their wishes and compete for the budget for its implementation. For every user request, the system chooses the most appropriate video plot and from the google image search a photo which would look the best when placed over the video. During the work with the project, we encountered an unexpected problem. The site was so popular that we instantly exhausted the limits of a free Google account. So we had to develop a caching and search in it in no time.

The project is made using Adobe Flash technology.
