catch a wave of freshness

http://wave.chezzmusic.com

Task

Develop a promotional website for the campaign ”Catch a wave of freshness“ for brand Chezzz. Reach maximum sharing in social networks.

Solution

The user was offered to upload an own photo and play a game trying to follow the drum or a guitar part of the music as accurate as possible. As an award, the user got a musical clip with him as one of the musicians.

In the video, I inserted a smoothed 3D model of a human head with applied a generated from the user photo texture. The texture was generated in the following way: eyes and mouth are found using openCV, the color gamma of the photo shifted to become close to the original texture, then the photo was placed above the original texture and on top of that the correction layer was placed. Later you could adjust the texture.

Late the user could share in social networks the proof that he or she participated in the musical clip.

The project was developed using Flash technology.
